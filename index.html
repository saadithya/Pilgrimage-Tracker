Track your spiritual journeys across India and globally

About the Extension

Pilgrimage Tracker helps you follow spiritual routes such as Char Dham, Jyotirlingas, Ashta Vinayak, and more. Get location-based alerts, track your progress, and explore maps powered by OpenStreetMap.

Features

  • Location-based reminders when near pilgrimage sites
  • Track progress and mark visits complete
  • Beautiful map integration
  • Optional premium pilgrimages with license key support
